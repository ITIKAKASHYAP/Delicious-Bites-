Our Story

Founded in 2010, Delicious Bites began as a small family-owned bistro with a passion for creating memorable dining experiences. What started as a humble 10-table restaurant has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations, thanks to our commitment to quality, innovation, and authentic Indian hospitality.

Our executive chef, Rohit Sharma, brings over 20 years of culinary expertise from various regions of India, crafting a menu that blends traditional Indian techniques with modern presentation. We take pride in supporting local farmers and spice merchants, ensuring the freshest ingredients and authentic flavors make it to your plate.
